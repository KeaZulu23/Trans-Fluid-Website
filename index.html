Why Choose Transfluid Engineering?

Trans-Fluid Engineering (TFE) was founded in 2020, bringing together a team with over 13 years of maintenance and engineering/technical experience, specializing in a wide variety of turbo machinery from various OEMs. Our foundation is built on efficiency and reliability, providing agility and flexibility to meet and exceed customer expectations. At TFE, we strive to maximize value for our customers by delivering quality management systems and project management capabilities to optimize schedules and costs while adhering to quality requirements and specifications.

Services

Services

At Transfluid Engineering, we offer a comprehensive range of specialized services to meet your diverse industrial needs.

Engineering Solutions

Pump Supply and Refurbishment

Whether you require a new pump or refurbishment of an existing one, we provide top-notch solutions. Our services encompass the supply of high-quality pumps and the meticulous refurbishment of worn components, ensuring efficiency and reliability.

Asset Integrity Studies and Improvement Programs

We conduct thorough asset integrity studies to identify areas for improvement. Our programs are designed to enhance the overall performance and longevity of your assets, contributing to increased efficiency and reduced downtime.

Non-Destructive Testing (NDT) on Refurbished and Manufactured Components

Rigorous NDT ensures the structural integrity of both refurbished and newly manufactured components. Our testing methods guarantee the highest quality standards, providing you with confidence in the reliability of your equipment.

Compressor Refurbishment and Balancing

Trust us for the refurbishment and precision balancing of compressors. Our services focus on extending the lifespan of your compressors while ensuring optimal performance and energy efficiency.

Implementation of ISO Quality System for Rotating Equipment

We specialize in implementing ISO quality systems specifically tailored for rotating equipment. This ensures that your equipment meets international quality standards, promoting consistency and reliability in operations.

Development of Quality Control Plans (QCPs) and Database Maintenance

Our team is dedicated to creating detailed Quality Control Plans (QCPs) to guide every step of the manufacturing and refurbishment process. We also maintain a comprehensive database to track and manage quality-related information.

Implementation of Non-Conformance (NCR) Management Processes

We have robust processes in place to manage non-conformances effectively. Our approach ensures timely identification, resolution, and continuous improvement, minimizing disruptions to your operations.

Creation and Maintenance of Drawings Database – 3D Measurement and Drafting Services

Count on us for accurate 3D measurements and drafting services. We create and maintain a comprehensive drawings database, facilitating precision in manufacturing, refurbishment, and equipment modifications.

Troubleshooting
